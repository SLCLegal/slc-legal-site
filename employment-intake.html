Start with the issue.

Plaintiff-side employment cases turn on what happened, when it happened, who was involved, and what records exist. This intake is designed to get those facts in one place quickly.

You do not need perfect dates, complete records, or every detail before you submit. Move through the selections, answer what you can, and keep going.

What matters from the start

  • Clear facts about what happened
  • Clear timing about what changed and when
  • Clear wage, record, and witness information where available

What clients do not need

  • Exact legal terminology
  • Perfect dates or amounts
  • Every document before submitting the intake
