Practice Areas

Employment and personal injury, both visible from the start.

Plaintiff-side representation requires clear facts, clear harm, and clear next steps. Both pathways should be legible immediately.

Employment

Wrongful Termination
Claims involving termination after protected complaints, leave, accommodation requests, wage complaints, or other contested employer conduct.
Retaliation
Representation when an employee speaks up, complains, takes leave, or asserts rights and then faces discipline, isolation, demotion, or termination.
Discrimination and Harassment
Claims involving disability, pregnancy, race, sex, age, religion, national origin, sexual orientation, gender identity, and other protected categories.
Wage and Hour
Unpaid wages, overtime, meal and rest break violations, final pay issues, misclassification, and wage statement claims.
Leave and Accommodation
Cases involving disability, medical condition, pregnancy, work restrictions, leave requests, return-to-work disputes, and failures in the interactive process.

Personal Injury

Car Crashes
Claims arising from collisions that leave clients dealing with injury, treatment, lost time, and insurer resistance.
Slip and Fall
Premises cases involving unsafe conditions, notice issues, and the real impact of an injury that should not be brushed aside.
Premises Liability
Claims involving unsafe property conditions, preventable hazards, and injury caused by failures in maintenance, warning, or control.
Serious Injury Claims
Cases where the injury changes mobility, work, daily life, or future care needs in a way that demands careful proof and presentation.
Insurance Disputes
Representation when carriers minimize, delay, dispute, or undervalue claims that deserve to be taken seriously.
