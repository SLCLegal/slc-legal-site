Zachary Stoltz

Zachary Stoltz represents employees because he knows how quickly the balance of power changes when a paycheck, leave request, medical issue, or complaint is used against the person who raised it.

His work focuses on retaliation, discrimination, harassment, wrongful termination, wage and hour disputes, PAGA matters, leave and accommodation cases, arbitration proceedings, and employment contract disputes. He also handles personal injury matters, including auto accidents and premises-liability claims.

He looks closely at the timeline, the documents, and the people involved. That is often where the real case begins.

Common case types

Tell us which problem sounds most like yours.

If more than one applies, that is common. Start with the one that feels most important.

Retaliation and wrongful termination You complained, requested leave, asked for accommodation, reported a problem, or spoke up about pay, and the employer turned against you.
Discrimination and harassment Pregnancy, disability, race, sex, age, national origin, religion, medical condition, or another protected basis changed how you were treated.
Wage and hour Unpaid overtime, missed meal or rest breaks, late final pay, wage statement problems, or company-wide pay practices affecting multiple workers.
Leave and accommodation You requested leave, provided a doctor’s note, asked for restrictions or schedule changes, and the employer ignored, delayed, denied, or punished you.
Whistleblower and Labor Code retaliation You reported unlawful conduct, safety issues, fraud, patient-care concerns, or wage violations and were disciplined, pushed out, or terminated.
Personal injury Auto accidents, slip and fall incidents, or other injury matters where liability, treatment, and damages need to be taken seriously from the start.
