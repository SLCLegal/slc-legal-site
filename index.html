Practice Areas

Structured for serious legal representation.

Short, consumer-facing summaries clarify scope without drifting into soft reassurance language or generic filler.

Practice Area

Wrongful Termination

Representation in termination matters involving retaliation, discrimination, leave, accommodation, protected complaints, and contested employer narratives.

Practice Area

Discrimination and Harassment

Claims involving protected categories and documented workplace conduct that gives rise to liability.

Practice Area

Wage and Hour

Unpaid wages, overtime, meal and rest break violations, final pay issues, misclassification, and wage statement claims.

Practice Area

Leave and Accommodation

Cases involving disability, pregnancy, medical condition, restrictions, leave requests, return-to-work disputes, or interactive-process failures.

Practice Area

Whistleblower and Retaliation

Claims involving internal complaints, safety concerns, labor complaints, compliance reports, and adverse action that follows.

Practice Area

Select Personal Injury

A secondary practice for serious injury matters where liability proof, treatment record, and damages presentation matter from the outset.

Why this structure works

A clearer hierarchy surfaces the branding instead of burying it.

The firm name and jurisdictions sit in a restrained, premium masthead instead of competing with repeated intake prompts.
The hero opens with positioning, not a generic slogan or wellness-adjacent reassurance language.
Employment remains primary, with personal injury present but clearly secondary.
The page uses fewer, better-placed calls to action so the brand feels more expensive and controlled.

  1. Premium masthead with SLC Legal, jurisdictions, and one primary intake action.
