Take it Back. Start with the incident.

Personal injury cases turn on what happened, where it happened, how injury occurred, what treatment followed, and what records or coverage exist. This intake is designed to get those facts in one place quickly.

You do not need perfect dates, full medical terminology, or every insurance detail before you submit. Move through the selections, answer what you can, and keep going.

What matters from the start

  • Clear incident facts and timing
  • Clear liability and notice facts
  • Clear injury, treatment, and coverage information

What clients do not need

  • Perfect medical terminology
  • Every bill, record, or photo before starting
  • Exact insurance information before beginning
